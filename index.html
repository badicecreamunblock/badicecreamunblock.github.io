Game Description: Bad Ice-Cream

Bad Ice Cream is famous for its popularity in the game world. To start this game, click on the play text after the Click to lick text, choose to play single or double, choose your character, choose a section and start the game. Get started before the ice cream melts and don't forget to play this wonderful game that has become widespread for 2 players.

On the next page, you start playing the Bad Ice Cream game unblocked by choosing one of the cocoa, strawberry and vanilla ice creams.

There are 40 sections in total. In each section, you need to collect all the fruits that appear on the screen to complete the section. Some fruits are fixed between the ice blocks and some are moving. In each section, you need to collect 2 or more types of fruits. The pictures of the fruits to be collected are visible in the window at the bottom of the screen. The fruits are arranged in order between the ice blocks. When a type of fruit is completely collected, the next type of fruit is placed between the blocks. The collected type of fruit disappears from the window at the bottom. While walking between the ice blocks, you should not be caught by the enemies protecting the fruits. If you get caught by the enemies, you burn and in this case, you have to start the same section again. Enemies consist of creatures such as cows, squids, trolls, log men, ducks and cannon towers. Each enemy has a different feature.

There are also objects such as hot stones, arrow tiles, campfires and invisible stones in the area. If your character hits these objects, you will burn.

How to Play Bad Ice Cream Unblocked?

The joyful Bad ice cream game can be played by one or two players. You can choose the single player version by clicking the 1 PLAYER button on the menu page, and the 2 player version by clicking the 2 PLAYERS button. In single player, the "W, A, S, D" or arrow keys are used, in 2 player, the 1st person uses the arrow keys, and the 2nd person uses the "A, S, D, W" keys. You break the ice blocks and open a path for yourself with the "SPACE" space and "F" key. You can block the path by placing ice blocks with the same keys.
